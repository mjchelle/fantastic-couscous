Section Optimization Tool


Automate TA Assignment Process For Efficiency

This is a tool that could import a CSV with information about TAs and filter them based on availability and prioritized criteria chosen by the instructor/coordinator. The TA information will be obtained through surveys.

Our product will appropriately weigh the importance of the students’ needs against the traits of the TAs. It will also place more importance on the TAs’ availability.
